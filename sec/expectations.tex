\subsection{Directed Studies and Thesis research}

Unlike typical courses you will not have formal evaluations (papers, exams) that provide feedback on your progress. However, I am required to provide a grade for these to count toward credit. Scientific research is a non-linear process, so your grade will not be based off of completion of research products but rather effort and persistence toward achieving those. You will earn an A by consistently spending (on average) 3 hours per week per credit hour (footnote with examples) \emph{substantively} pursuing research goals. Note the term substantively: this means that during those 3 hours you are working efficiently, thinking and reflecting critically on your work, and proactively addressing problems/issues as they arise. During our regularly scheduled meetings, we will have one-on-one progress check meetings at $\frac{1}{3}$ intervals of the term to reflect on research approaches, research progress, and grade expectations.
